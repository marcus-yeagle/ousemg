About

The best group on campus.

The Ohio University Student Equity Management Group manages an equity portfolio of roughly $4.7 million (4/2014) of the university's assets under a concentrated portfolio strategy. It is directed at generating consistent risk-adjusted returns in excess of broader equity benchmarks.The Group, founded in late 2001, was allocated its first funds in March of 2002. Since inception, OUSEMG has generated approximately $300,000 (06/2008) in capital gains and $47,104 of dividend income, which supports scholarships, research grants, and other initiatives of the Ohio University Foundation.

Our Mission

OUSEMG seeks to cultivate a highly engaging academic society and culture that challenges its members in areas of advanced financial topics. Further, the Group seeks to leverage its investment directive to pursue value-adding activities directed at the university, its members, and its alumni.
