Student Portfolio Management

The Ohio University Student Equity Management Group is a student organization that manages an equity portfolio of $4.7 million of the university’s assets under a concentrated portfolio strategy.

The Group seeks to use the management of the equity portfolio as a common purpose under which we seek to provide our members with the required skills and knowledge for placement into highly competitive internships and full-time positions.

About

OU Student Equity Management Group manages an equity portfolio of roughly $4.7 million of the university's assets under a concentrated portfolio strategy.

Performance

OUSEMG measures our performance by constantly evaluating our ability to fulfill our three goals: adding value to our members, adding value to our university, adding value to our alumni.

Members

Our members receive the skills necessary to gain employment with many of the most respected firms across the financial services industry and beyond. We measure our performance in this area by seeking to achieve 100% senior placement by graduation.

Contact

Interested in joining? We accept new applicants for our internship program every semester. Sign up now for the Fall 2016 semester. The group is open to all colleges and majors at Ohio University.
